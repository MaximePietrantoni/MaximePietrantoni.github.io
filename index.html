I am a PhD student in the Computer Science doctoral study program at the Department of Cybernetics, Faculty of Electrical Engineering (FEE) at Czech Technical University (CTU) and affiliated to Naver Labs Europe. My PhD topic is Representation Learning for Robust and Privacy Preserving Visual Localization under the co-supervision of Torsten Sattler, Gabriela Csurka and Martin Humenberger. My main research interests are in visual localization, representation learning, scene understanding and 3D reconstruction. I received my engineering degree from CentraleSupélec (France) majoring in applied mathematics. In parallel I obtained the MVA (Mathematics, Vision and Learning) master degree from Ecole Normale Supérieure Paris-Saclay (France) in applied mathematics, deep learning and computer vision. Before my doctoral studies, I interned at Naver Labs Europe, working on sim-to-real transfer for localization and navigation tasks.
